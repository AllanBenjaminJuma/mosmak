Tour Overview

This 8-day Kenya Safari takes you through the country’s most celebrated wildlife destinations. From the rolling plains of the Maasai Mara to the flamingo-filled shores of Lake Nakuru and the majestic views of Mount Kilimanjaro in Amboseli, this journey delivers the ultimate African safari experience.

Travel in a comfortable 4x4 safari vehicle with expert local guides who know the land intimately. Enjoy daily game drives, breathtaking landscapes, rich wildlife encounters, and cultural interactions with the Maasai community.

Tour Highlights

  • Big Five Wildlife Viewing
  • 4x4 Safari Land Cruiser
  • Professional English-speaking Guide
  • Full Board Accommodation
  • Maasai Cultural Visit
  • Scenic Rift Valley Viewpoints

Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1

Nairobi to Maasai Mara

Depart Nairobi in the morning and drive through the scenic Great Rift Valley to Maasai Mara National Reserve. Afternoon game drive in search of lions, elephants, giraffes, and more.

Welcome Dinner
Day 2-3

Full Safari Experience in Maasai Mara

Spend two full days exploring the vast plains of the Maasai Mara. Track the Big Five and witness the Great Migration (seasonal). Optional hot air balloon safari available.

Breakfast
Day 4

Lake Nakuru National Park

Travel to Lake Nakuru, home to flamingos, rhinos, and leopards. Enjoy an afternoon game drive and stunning lake views.

Breakfast
Days 5-6

Amboseli & Mount Kilimanjaro Views

Explore Amboseli National Park, famous for large elephant herds and spectacular views of Mount Kilimanjaro. Enjoy sunrise and sunset game drives.

Breakfast, Cooking Class
Days 7

Maasai Cultural Experience

Visit a traditional Maasai village and learn about their customs, traditions, and way of life.

Breakfast Daily
Days 8

Return to Nairobi

After breakfast, drive back to Nairobi with unforgettable safari memories.

Breakfast, Farewell Dinner
Day 12

Departure

Transfer to Istanbul Airport for your departure flight. Take home memories of an incredible Mediterranean adventure that will last a lifetime.

Breakfast
