Tour Overview

Experience the unmatched beauty of the Masai Mara with Mosmak Tours & Travel. This safari takes you deep into Kenya’s most celebrated wildlife reserve, offering breathtaking landscapes, thrilling game drives, and unforgettable encounters with nature.

Our safaris are designed to suit your preferences—whether you're seeking adventure, photography opportunities, or a relaxing escape in the wild. Every detail is carefully planned to ensure a seamless and enriching safari experience.

Safari Highlights

  • Big Five Game Drives
  • Expert Safari Guides
  • Luxury & Midrange Lodges
  • Great Migration (Seasonal)
  • Cultural Maasai Visits
  • Optional Balloon Safari

Sample Safari Itinerary

Day 1

Arrival & Transfer to Masai Mara

Depart from Nairobi and enjoy a scenic drive to the Masai Mara. Arrive at your lodge or camp in time for lunch, followed by an afternoon game drive.

Day 2

Full Day Game Drives

Spend the day exploring the vast plains of the Masai Mara. Track the Big Five and enjoy close encounters with wildlife in their natural habitat.

Day 3

Optional Activities & Exploration

Choose from optional experiences such as a hot air balloon safari or a cultural visit to a Maasai village. Afternoon game drive continues.

Day 4

Return to Nairobi

Enjoy a morning game drive before departing back to Nairobi with unforgettable safari memories.

What's Included

  • Transport in safari vehicle
  • Professional safari guide
  • Accommodation (lodges/camps)
  • Park entry fees
  • Game drives
  • Meals as per itinerary

What's Not Included

  • Personal expenses
  • Travel insurance
  • Tips and gratuities
  • Optional activities (balloon safari, etc.)
