Essential Travel Information

Everything you need to know for your Kenyan adventure

Best Time to Visit

July to October for wildlife safaris. December to March for coastal and beach adventures.

Weather & Climate

Tropical climate on the coast, temperate in highlands, and semi-arid in the savannah regions.

Currency & Language

Kenyan Shilling (KES) is the official currency. English and Swahili are widely spoken.

Visa Requirements

Most travelers require a valid passport and eVisa, which can be obtained online before arrival.

Local Tips & Cultural Insights

  • Book safari accommodations early, especially during peak season
  • Wear comfortable walking shoes for city tours and nature walks
  • Carry sun protection—Kenya can be very sunny
  • Respect local customs and dress codes, especially in rural areas
  • Try local dishes like ugali, nyama choma, and fresh seafood
  • Use local guides to enrich cultural and wildlife experiences
